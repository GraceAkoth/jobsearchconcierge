Questions

Frequently Asked Questions.

Do you guarantee I will get a job in 3 months?
I do not guarantee a job offer — no ethical career professional can. What I guarantee is a fully executed, professional job search campaign. Most clients begin receiving interview invitations within the first 4–6 weeks. Some secure offers within the 3-month window; others take a few weeks beyond. The campaign continues to work after the programme ends because of the positioning we build together.
How much of my time is required each week?
Typically 2–3 hours per week — primarily your weekly strategy call, reviewing any materials I have prepared, and attending interviews as they come in. I handle all the behind-the-scenes work so your time investment is minimal but always purposeful.
Do you need access to my LinkedIn account?
Yes. To send messages, submit applications, and manage outreach on your behalf, I will need temporary login access to your LinkedIn account. All activity is conducted professionally and with your full visibility. You remain in control at all times.
What types of roles do you specialise in?
I work primarily with senior managers, directors, heads of department, and C-suite executives across industries including FMCG, technology, financial services, media, NGOs, and professional services across Africa.
Can I pay in instalments?
Yes. A split payment arrangement is available upon request — typically 50% at commencement and 50% at the start of Month 2. Please mention this when you apply and we will discuss what works for you.
How do I apply?
Email [email protected] with your name, current role, what you are looking to achieve, and your target timeline. I will review your application and respond within 48 hours. If there is a fit, we will schedule a brief discovery call before confirming your spot.
